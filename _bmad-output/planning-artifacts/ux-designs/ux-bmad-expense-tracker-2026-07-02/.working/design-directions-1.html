Design Directions — bmad-expense-tracker

Four visual directions for the same two screens (Dashboard, Quick Add), so Sam's calm-not-alarmed budgeting experience can be compared side by side before one direction is picked and folded into DESIGN.md tokens.

Each phone frame is ~375px wide (mobile-first). All amounts use ₹ INR. Green is the primary budget state shown on the main Dashboard mock; amber/red variants of the same status component are shown as insets. Quick Add shows the required-description gating on the save button (disabled vs. enabled) and one always-on focus-ring example on the description field.

A. Calm Harbor

calm blue-teal · airy · soft shadows · rounded friendly sans

A calm blue palette with soft-shadow white cards and generous spacing keeps the interface feeling airy and reassuring rather than clinical. Rounded 16px corners and a friendly system sans-serif avoid any sense of institutional banking software. Every status color is paired with an icon and bold factual text so meaning never depends on hue alone.

B. Warm Ledger

warm cream-terracotta · flat bordered · denser · serif numerals

A warm cream-and-terracotta register with flat, 1px-bordered cards feels like a tidy paper ledger rather than a cold banking dashboard, softening the anxiety of budgeting. Slightly denser spacing and serif headline numerals give amounts a grounded, considered weight while staying easy to scan. Traffic-light states use warm-toned tints so red and amber read as informative, not alarming.

C. Quiet Slate

cool slate-graphite · crisp neutral sans · flat tint blocks · denser

A cool slate-and-graphite base with flat tinted card blocks — no borders or shadows — reads as crisp and neutral, favoring information density for someone who wants to scan totals fast. Tight letter-spacing on headings and a plain system sans keep the tone matter-of-fact rather than playful. Status tints stay muted and are always paired with an icon and exact copy so severity reads without feeling like an alarm.

D. Fresh Mint Minimal

lavender-mint accent · extra-rounded · bold headings · airy

An extra-rounded lavender-and-mint register with bold headings and larger tap targets is the most overtly friendly direction, aimed squarely at reassuring an anxious first-time budgeter. Soft shadows and generous spacing keep it light and encouraging rather than dense or transactional. The accent color stays purely decorative — budget severity is still carried only by the green/amber/red status tints, icons and copy, never by the brand color.
